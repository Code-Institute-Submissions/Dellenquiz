Dellenquiz

What's about and how to play

Dellenquiz is a promotion of the surroundings of both the North and South Dellen. You will find interesting historical geographical facts, interesting places to visit or just good to know information. As you already know the Dellendistrict has two major lakes and around these lakes is a fantastic nature, placed, events and people

You can test your knowledge or even find new knowledge depending on how much you know about the Dellendistrict. When clicking on the start button you start and will get the a question with four (4) choices, if nothing else is mentioned then only one choice is correct. You will not get a feedback on if it is correct or false right away. Click on the choice you think is correct, press Next button and so on until the last question. When you made it to the end of the quiz press on the Submit button and you will get your results. Hopefully you made the right choices.
